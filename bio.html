James Coats Bio

James Coats is an entrepreneur, consultant, and business expert who is passionate about improving professional and personal performance. His mission is to empower organizations to develop the capabilities needed to perform at their best by building systems to capitalize on opportunities and reduce market ineffectiveness.

He is the Founder & CEO of Prolific Touch, a marketing consulting firm that primarily serves the entertainment, technology, and business sectors. He partners with clients to improve organizational performance, culture, leadership, teamwork, entrepreneurial thinking, creative strategy, and growth hacking. With over 10 years of experience in marketing and administration, he has expertise in the areas of management, marketing, sales, branding, design, leadership, team development, strategic planning, entrepreneurship, and public speaking. He is passionate about helping people and organizations perform beyond expectations by building the capabilities needed for future growth amidst a rapidly changing business environment.

James earned his Masters in Business Administration from California State University, San Bernardino with a concentration in Entrepreneurship Management. He received his B.A. in Marketing Management with a concentration on brand development as well as a B.F.A. in Graphic Design from California State Polytechnic University Pomona. Both programs emphasized a hands-on approach to learning, which allowed him to lead many diverse academic teams in real world business projects. James is currently working on publishing his first book, A Simple Game: The Formula for Phenomenal Performance. He teaches courses on management, entrepreneurship, marketing, leadership, strategic planning, and organizational development online and around the world through the BizNest Network.

James has worked in the public and private sectors as well as working with multiple nonprofits on business development, strategic marketing, branding, and improving their professional identity. He resides in Southern California with his son, as a person that champions growth, James is constantly looking for new ways to spur development both personally and professionally by tackling new and challenging interest. This affords him a unique perspective and method to connect with his work and clients.
